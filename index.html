Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before joining DevConf 2026.

🚀 Is DevConf 2026 only for experienced developers?

Not at all. DevConf 2026 is designed for everyone, from beginners taking their first steps in programming to experienced developers looking to explore new technologies, share knowledge, and connect with the global developer community.

🤖 Will AI change the future of software development?

Artificial Intelligence is transforming the way developers build software. At DevConf 2026, experts will discuss how AI can improve productivity, create smarter applications, and help developers adapt to the future of technology instead of replacing creativity and innovation.

💡 Can I showcase my own project at DevConf?

Absolutely! DevConf 2026 encourages developers and students to showcase their creative solutions, personal projects, and innovative ideas. It is a great opportunity to receive feedback, gain visibility, and connect with people who share the same passion for technology.

🌎 What opportunities can I get beyond the sessions?

Beyond technical sessions, DevConf provides opportunities to meet industry professionals, startup founders, recruiters, and fellow developers. These connections can lead to collaborations, career opportunities, mentorship, and valuable insights about the technology industry.

⚡ Will there be coding challenges or hackathons?

Yes, participants will have opportunities to take part in coding challenges, collaborative activities, and hackathon-style events. These experiences allow developers to test their skills, solve real-world problems, and work together to create impactful solutions.

🎯 Why should I attend DevConf 2026?

DevConf 2026 is more than just a technology event. It is a platform where developers can discover emerging trends, learn from industry experts, build meaningful connections, and get inspired to create the next generation of digital solutions.
